About Us

Financial literacy is a significant issue in the United States, affecting individuals ability to manage their finances. Without proper financial management skills, many people fall into cycles of debt and financial instability, making it crucial to address this widespread issue immediately. Budget Buddy offers an innovative solution to the financial challenges faced by many Americans. By offering real-time insights and goal-setting tools, Budget Buddy not only helps users better understand their spending habits but also guides them in making informed financial decisions
